# Design Rationale – Glow Salon Appointment Booking App

## Problem Statement
Many local salons still depend on phone calls and messaging applications for appointment management, leading to scheduling confusion and poor customer experience.

## Solution
A mobile appointment booking application that enables customers to select services, choose specialists, schedule appointments, and receive booking confirmation quickly.

## Target Users
- Salon customers
- Busy professionals
- First-time visitors
- Regular salon clients

## Booking Flow

Splash Screen
↓
Home Screen
↓
Service Selection
↓
Specialist Selection
↓
Date & Time Booking
↓
Appointment Confirmation

## UX Decisions

### Easy Navigation
A step-by-step booking flow reduces confusion.

### Touch-Friendly Design
Large buttons and cards improve mobile usability.

### Visual Consistency
A soft pink and white color palette matches salon branding.

### Booking Confirmation
Users receive clear appointment details after booking.

## Real-World Benefits

### For Customers
- Faster booking
- Better experience
- Reduced waiting time

### For Business
- Organized scheduling
- Reduced missed appointments
- Improved customer satisfaction

## Conclusion
The proposed application provides a modern, efficient, and user-friendly appointment booking experience while helping the business manage appointments effectively.
